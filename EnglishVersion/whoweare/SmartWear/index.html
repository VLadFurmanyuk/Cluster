Smart-Wear Lab

An inter-cluster project on the development and production of Smart clothing both for the Ukrainian market and for export.

It is a garment that connects innovative technologies of digital monitoring of human activities through cloud data collected by micro-sensors; and/or changing the physical performance of clothing (for example, heating) through control from a mobile device

At the first stage, we developed clothing with climate control controlled from a mobile phone. The result of the development was presented for the first time at the "East-Expo 2022" forum in Kyiv.

Project participants: PA "East Ukrainian Technocluster" (Kramatorsk) – IT part of the project; PA "Donbas Fashion Cluster" in the person of Velna (tm) enterprise (Ukrainian city) - tailoring; PA "Agency for Sustainable Development and Innovation" (Bakhmut) - project management;
