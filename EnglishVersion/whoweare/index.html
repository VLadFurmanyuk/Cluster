Cash registers for the masses

Eastern Ukrainian Technocluster has launched the project "Cash registers for the masses".. The main
goal of the project is to ensure the transparency of doing business by micro- and small enterprises through the use of Program RSOs, in accordance with the changes to the Tax Code of Ukraine that came into effect in 2022. We conducted an in-depth analysis of existing products on the PRSO market. As a result, a comparative table of advantages and disadvantages of each product was formed. Recommendations were developed for businesses of various profiles on the implementation of PRSO technologies.

Temporary Relocation of the Cluster to Lviv

The Russian-Ukrainian war turned out to be a serious factor for business and the general life of the
country. Already from the first day of the war, the native city of Kramatorsk was subjected to ever-
increasing enemy shelling. Therefore, for the sake of the safety of the members of the "Eastern
Ukrainian Technocluster", the management made a decision to temporarily relocate to the city of Lviv.
The team with their families, equipment, computer equipment were transported.
From the end of March, despite its name, the "Southern Ukrainian Technocluster" PA began working in
the western region of Ukraine.

Development of smart specialization of the Donetsk
region. in the direction of Mechanical Engineering and Information Technologies

We took part in the meeting of the working group on the development of smart specialization of the
Donetsk region in the direction of "Mechanical engineering using modern information technologies
(IT)" as part of the "Transformation of regions through joint smart solutions" project. The meeting was
held in Kramatorsk and Mariupol, and online communication was established between the cities.
Together with the experts of the Ukrainian Institute of International Policy, the Joint Research Center,
the EU Science Center and other students of the working group, they formed a SWOT matrix of the
Donetsk region at the intersection of two industries: Mechanical Engineering and Information
Technologies.

Smart-Wear Lab

An inter-cluster project on the development and production of Smart clothing both for the Ukrainian market and for export. It is a garment that connects innovative technologies of digital monitoring of
human activities through cloud data collected by micro-sensors; and/or changing the physical
performance of clothing (for example, heating) through control from a mobile device

At the first stage, we developed clothing with climate control controlled from a mobile phone. The
result of the development was presented for the first time at the "East-Expo 2022" forum in Kyiv.

Development of the Kramatorsk Progress Strategy
urban territorial community

PA "East Ukrainian Technocluster" takes an active part in the development of the strategy of the
Kramatorsk Territorial Community, which will determine its progress vector for the next 6 years, and
which will be completed already in December
A city or community strategy is a long-term planning practice common in the world, which helps to
analyze the problems and opportunities of the region, determine the priority directions of
development - for example, social infrastructure, tourism or support of entrepreneurs - and implement
projects more efficiently.
