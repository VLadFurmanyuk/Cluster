Eastern Ukrainian Technocluster - who are we?

ETC is a community of small and medium-sized companies in the fields of information technology, education and industry.
The territory of our influence is Donetsk and Luhansk regions
Sustainable development of the IT industry and modern technologies in
region by building a full-fledged ecosystem
"IT - Business - Government - Education"

Our goals

Implementation of joint business projects in the field of IT and modern technologies

Development of community fundraising, attraction of investments in joint projects

Creation and promotion of a platform for Startups with the aim of achieving overall financial success

Implementation of Smart technologies in the region, improvement of digital interaction of society and authorities

Increasing the competitiveness of the region's IT community on the world market

Creation of financially successful Products that will be competitive on the world market and in Ukraine

Improving and expanding the competence of the community of the region with the help of specialized educational programs and courses

Support for sustainable association of companies and individuals persons
