Cash registers to the masses

Eastern Ukrainian Technocluster has launched the project "Cash registers to the masses". The main goal of the project is to provide micro-small and medium-sized businesses with RROs in accordance with the Tax Code of Ukraine.

We conducted an in-depth analysis of existing products in the PRSO market. As a result, a table of disadvantages and advantages of each product was formed, recommendations were developed for enterprises of different profiles for the implementation of PRSO technologies.

More than 150 entrepreneurs from Donetsk and Luhansk regions have joined our Cluster project, and PRSOs are finally being used to run their business.

This activity was carried out under the mentorship of members of the Cluster of companies "A-SOFT PRO" and "Key-X".
