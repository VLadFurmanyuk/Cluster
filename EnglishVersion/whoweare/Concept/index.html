The concept of increasing the economic and security independence of Ukraine "1C follows the russian ship"

The concept of increasing the economic and security independence of Ukraine "1C follows the Russian ship" was developed by the cluster

Description of the problem 1. In Ukraine, according to some data, 800,000 1C installations (BAS is a 1C clone to hide from the 1C brand);

2. The 1C program really “spills” the data of Ukrainian enterprises “on Lubyanka” - this should be taken as an axiom, even if we do not have direct evidence;

3. The 1C program is not under a direct ban, but its largest integrators are on the sanctions lists according to the decrees of the President of Ukraine No. 133/2017, No. 126/2018, No. 184/2020 and the Law of Ukraine "On Sanctions" No. 1644-VII of 14- 08-2014;

4. The conditional ban on the use of budget funds for 1C leads to the fact that enterprises are forced to hide the costs of its maintenance under fictitious articles of the KEKV (repair of equipment, refilling cartridges, etc.), that is, they deliberately violate the legislation of Ukraine;

5. 1C is a product of intellectual property, which means that it has certain license deductions for rights holders. Which have as final beneficiaries natural persons of the aggressor country. That is, the fee for the use of 1C is actually the financing of terrorism;

6. There is no single strategy in the country regarding the strict prohibition of 1C, because it will lead to the simultaneous collapse of small businesses;

7. The lack of "hygiene" regarding 1C does not allow domestic analogues to develop, and makes competition with 1C economically unjustified, and domestic software products cannot achieve the same distribution;

8. There is a precedent of issuing an order from the KRU (State Audit Service) regarding the ban on the use of 1C at budget institutions, that is, it is only a matter of time when there will be full-fledged pressure on russian programs on the ground;

9. Commercial, budget, utility enterprises, despite all the issues, actively use russian software products; The assistance of GS "Eastern Ukrainian Technocluster" consists of the following:

We offer the transition of commercial, communal, budget enterprises to a domestic software product. A number of such products are presented on the software market of Ukraine. But each of them has its limitations in terms of complexity, availability of technically and professionally experienced personnel.

Example: IS-Pro – is an extremely complex product that requires the presence of dedicated personnel (programmers) in the company's staff. But they have a similar business in the Russian Federation;

MasterBuh — is a very high-quality development, but maintenance is conducted centrally at the country level, local solutions are expected for a long time;

Parus, also known as Athena - is a complex solution, but also has Russian roots, was on the sanctions list for three years, therefore lost a lot of its potential;

M.E.Doc - it can be called an accounting program only conditionally. There is no professional technical support. It has a negative background due to the NePetya virus infection of many businesses both in the country and abroad (for example, Maersk).

The software product of the Cluster member - the "AREAL: ACCOUNT" program has been tested by time and many customers. The Areal program integrates with the Art-Report reporting program. That is, enterprises still save money on reporting programs.

Local integrator enterprises have Ukrainian roots, which means that the funds will remain in the community of the city (region), and not go to the aggressor country to pay the franchise. For this purpose, PA "Eastern Ukrainian technocluster" helps to build professional teams and business processes and training of partner enterprises;

The cluster is developing measures to train specialists specifically to work with domestic software. In particular, we are preparing a training program for specialists specializing in "Accounting" for universities and employment centers based on the Areal-Accounting program;

We cooperate with integrators of various cities of Ukraine (clusters, associations) to gradually replace their 1C products with the Areal program in their product line.

We are developing promotional activities among the members of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Ukraine and for interested persons, which would enable domestic enterprises to understand the advantages of Ukrainian software products and the importance of increasing Ukraine's economic and security independence.
